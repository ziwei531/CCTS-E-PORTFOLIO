Something I Am!

My first assignment introduced me to the concept of Design Thinking. It is a five-step process that consist of empathy, define, ideation, prototype, and presentation. Throughout this whole assignment process, I was constantly trying to improvise such as finding ways to further enhance my really bad drawings through utilizing the various other features that PowerPoint has to offer. Sometimes I would search the internet for certain effects and convert the backgrounds to become transparent and subsequently pasting it onto my rather dull stickman drawings in order to make it less dull looking. Although my final product is not very satisfactory, I have at least gained new wisdom in how to improve certain aspects of my future projects through what I have learned in design thinking.
