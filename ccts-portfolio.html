Taylor's Graduate Capabilities

The TGC, or Taylor's Graduate Capabilities, are values that aims to prepare us students to be more adaptive to the constant changing working environment.


The TGC values I have successfully gained from doing my CCTS assignments are:


  • Lifelong learning - Design thinking has opened my eyes to the possibilities in how I could further improve my projects in the future.
  • Thinking and problem solving - As I have been doing my assignments, I had to constantly improvise to make my creations more interesting as I am very terrible at drawing.
  • Intrapersonal Skills - As I was chosen to be the team leader for Design Your Own Country, I was tasked to motivate my teammates to do their work without too much delays and to ensure satisfactory performance were delivered from them.
  • Communication Skills - It is important that I maintain good communication with my teammates so as to reduce the amount misunderstandings that might arise while we were doing our group assignment.
