Design Your Own Country

This is a group assignment that had me paired up with two other people to work together with. This assignment is similar to the first assignment as it requires my group to utilize the concept of design thinking in order to create our very own unique country. As we were only given two weeks to complete this assignment, it was quite a hectic process and we were constantly struggling to come up with ideas in order to create a unique and intriguing country. During the whole process, we were once again utilizing the concept of design thinking to its fullest potential in which I believed had further increased everyone’s exposure and understanding of the concept of design thinking. As a result, we were able to create a rather exceptional country. Although due to a tight deadline, the end result wasn’t really adequate enough in my opinion but at least it was satisfactory in terms of how we managed to fully utilized design thinking in order to complete our assignment and that I have gained more insights in how useful design thinking can be.
