During the entire course of this module, I have truly gained a lot of new wisdom and knowledge. As I am terrible at drawing, I was forced to constantly improvise in order to make up on my lack of artistic skills for my assignments. Despite my terrible drawing, the things that I have learned throughout this module had been eye-opening and I really felt that I have benefitted greatly from doing CCTS.

Taylor's Graduate Capabilities

The TGC, or Taylor's Graduate Capabilities, are values that aims to prepare us students to be more adaptive to the constant changing working environment.


The TGC values I have successfully gained from doing my CCTS assignments are:


  • Lifelong learning - Design thinking has opened my eyes to the possibilities in how I could further improve my projects in the future.
  • Thinking and problem solving - When I was doing my assignments, I had to constantly improvise to make my creations more interesting as I am very terrible at drawing.
  • Intrapersonal Skills - As I was chosen to be the team leader for Design Your Own Country, I was tasked to motivate my teammates to do their work without too much delays and to ensure satisfactory performance was delivered from them.
  • Communication Skills - It is important that I maintain good communication with my teammates so as to reduce the amount of misunderstandings that might arise while we were doing our group assignment.

Final Thoughts

Overall the skills that I have learned in this module would most definitely assist me in my future endeavours especially in brainstorming for projects and how I could potentially improve it with the usage of design thinking. In fact, there are already occassions in my life right now that I have unconsciously been using what I have learned in this module to further improve certain personal projects of mine that I am working on right now and I am truly grateful to have taken this module!
